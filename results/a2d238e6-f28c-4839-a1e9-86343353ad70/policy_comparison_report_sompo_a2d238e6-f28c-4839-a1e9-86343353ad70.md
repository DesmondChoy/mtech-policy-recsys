**Justification:**

Comparing the SOMPO TravelJoy tiers (Vital, Deluxe, Elite) against the customer's requirements for a 10-day golfing trip to Australia (excluding the unsuitable GO Japan! tier):

*   **Elite:** Offers the highest core coverage limits ($1M medical, $15k cancellation, $8k luggage) and the highest limit for pre-existing conditions under Emergency Evacuation ($150k). Crucially, the Elite tier is the *only* tier that includes a comprehensive 'Golf Cover' section (Section 36) addressing *all* the customer's specific golf-related requests: Loss/damage to golf equipment ($1000), Hole-in-one benefit ($500), Unused green fees ($250), and Damage to buggy ($500).

Given the customer's strong emphasis on specific golf coverage elements and the desire for comprehensive protection (stating budget is flexible for good coverage), the Elite tier is the most suitable. The No Mix-and-Match rule prevents taking the golf benefits from Elite and applying them to a lower tier. Elite provides the complete package required, aligning best with the holistic needs expressed. The price tie-breaker rule was not required as Elite is demonstrably superior in meeting the specific requirements.
