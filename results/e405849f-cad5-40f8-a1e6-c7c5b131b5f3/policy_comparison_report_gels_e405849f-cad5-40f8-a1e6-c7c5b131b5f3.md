**Recommended Tier:** : Gold

**Justification:**

Comparing the GELS Basic, Gold, and Platinum tiers:
*   **Basic Tier:** Fails to meet several critical requirements. It explicitly excludes coverage for pre-existing conditions like asthma, does not cover adventurous leisure activities such as Scuba Diving, and lacks coverage for additional pet accommodation costs.
*   **Gold Tier:** Meets all core requirements *provided the optional Pre-existing conditions add-on benefit is purchased*. It includes coverage for adventurous leisure activities (Scuba Diving) as standard, covers Trip Cancellation, Travel Delay, and Lost/Damaged Luggage. Crucially, it offers Pet Care coverage for additional kennel costs due to delays, and the optional add-on addresses the asthma coverage need for medical expenses and trip disruptions (subject to add-on limits and co-payments).
*   **Platinum Tier:** Also meets all core requirements *provided the optional Pre-existing conditions add-on benefit is purchased*. It offers higher coverage limits than the Gold tier across most categories (Medical, Cancellation, Luggage, Pet Care) and includes additional benefits like a shorter trigger time for travel delay payouts and sports equipment cover.

**Reasoning for Recommendation:**
The Basic tier is unsuitable. Both Gold and Platinum tiers can meet the customer's requirements, but *only if the optional Pre-existing conditions add-on is purchased* to cover the asthma. The Gold tier (with the add-on) provides sufficient coverage limits ($500k Medical, $10k Cancellation, $5k Luggage, $500 Pet Care) to meet the customer's need for "proper cover" across all specified areas, including Scuba Diving and Pet Care. While Platinum offers higher limits, the customer did not explicitly request maximum coverage, only comprehensive protection for their stated concerns. Since both Gold (with add-on) and Platinum (with add-on) adequately address the essential requirements, the **Tie-Breaking Rule** is applied. Gold appears earlier in the price ranking (cheaper) than Platinum, making it the most suitable recommendation among the tiers that meet the core needs. The customer must be advised that purchasing the **Pre-existing conditions add-on benefit** is essential with the Gold plan to cover asthma-related issues.
