Comparing the SOMPO Vital, Deluxe, GO Japan!, and Elite tiers against the customer's requirements for a golf trip to Australia:

This tier fails to meet several key golf-specific requirements.

*   **Elite:** This tier provides the highest limits for core coverage like medical ($1M overseas), trip cancellation ($15k), and baggage ($8k). Given the No Mix-and-Match rule, the customer must select a single tier. Only the Elite tier provides coverage for the full suite of requirements, particularly the essential golf-specific benefits requested. Therefore, Elite is the most suitable recommendation despite potential partial limitations on equipment value and hole-in-one conditions.
